Support & FAQ

Welcome to our support page. Below are some frequently asked questions related to deposits, withdrawals, and investment returns.

How do I make a deposit? +

Deposits can be made in USD through our secure payment gateway. Simply navigate to the deposit section in your dashboard and follow the instructions to fund your account.

What is the withdrawal process? +

You can withdraw your funds into any local currency. The amount you wish to withdraw is entered in USD and our system automatically converts it to your preferred currency using the current market rate, minus any applicable fees.

How are my interest earnings calculated? +

Once you've signed up with Lexuexchange you get an interest of 85USD. Your interest is calculated daily on your investment portfolio and is credited to your interest wallet. The interest rate is dynamic and depends on market performance, ensuring you get amazing returns.

Is my investment secure? +

Yes, we use advanced security protocols to protect your funds and personal information. Our platform is built to provide a secure and reliable investment environment.
